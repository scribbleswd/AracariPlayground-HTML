Aracari Playground Features

Built for speed, conversion, and simplicity — no extra tooling required.

🎨

Tailwind CSS

Transform your template's appearance with the beloved Tailwind framework. Easily customize your design, bringing your unique vision to life.

📬

Contact Form

Supports topics, enabling submissions to go directly to Slack or services like SendGrid/Postmark. Assign different channels to each topic.

📰

Newsletters

Mailchimp integration empowers you to create multiple forms with robust tag and list support. Streamline your marketing effortlessly.

🧩

Page Block Builder

Craft your perfect page with a selection of versatile blocks. Customize and extend exactly the way you want it.

🔗

Git-Based CMS

With Static CMS, your content lives in a Git repository, ensuring efficient content management and seamless team collaboration.

🌓

Light & Dark Mode

Give your visitors the freedom to choose their preferred color scheme, ensuring a personalized browsing experience from day one.

Security

Secure by design

By eliminating databases and server-side code, static sites become less prone to attacks. Embrace the robust security of static architecture.

🔒
  • No database to breach or exploit
  • No server-side code execution
  • Edge-cached, globally distributed
  • Automatic HTTPS via Cloudflare
  • DDoS protection built-in
